Logical Model: CDA - assignedPerson

Official URL: https://interop.esante.gouv.fr/ig/document/core/StructureDefinition/fr-core-person Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2025-05-23 Computable Name: FrAssignedPerson

L’élément de l’en-tête du CDA assignedPerson permet de décrire une personne physique.

Usage:

  • Use this Logical Model: CDA - assignedAuthor, CDA - assignedEntity, CDA - associatedEntity, CDA - intendedRecipient and CDA - relatedEntity
